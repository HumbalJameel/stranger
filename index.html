Synopsis

After their car breaks down in an eerie small town, a young couple (Madelaine
Petsch and Froy Gutierrez) are forced to spend the night in a remote cabin.

Panic ensues as they are terrorized by three masked strangers who strike with no mercy and seemingly
no motive in THE STRANGERS ― CHAPTER 1, the chilling first entry of this upcoming horror feature film
series.
